We Are NYP SoundCard

A Family of Voices. A Passion for Music.

Want to Sing Like Your Favourite Artists?

We give you the tools, the community, and the stage to make it happen.

Our weekly sessions are a blend of structured training and relaxed, collaborative practice. Whether you're a beginner learning to hold a note or an experienced singer looking for a group to jam with, you'll find your place here.

  • Weekly Vocal Sessions

    Group training on technique, harmony, and performance.

  • Open Mic Nights

    A casual and supportive space to perform solo or with friends.

  • Campus Showcases

    Opportunities to perform at major school events like Open House.

Why Join Us?

Hone Your Craft

Receive vocal training, experiment with harmonies, and gain invaluable stage experience in a supportive environment.

Find Your Community

More than just a club, we're a close-knit family that rehearses, performs, and grows together.

Shine on Stage

From campus-wide showcases to intimate acoustic nights, you'll have numerous opportunities to perform.
