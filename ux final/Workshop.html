Our Workshops

Choose a path to elevate your vocal skills.

Beginner Vocals: Find Your Voice

This 4-week course is designed for absolute beginners. You'll learn the core mechanics of singing in a fun, no-pressure environment. We focus on building a strong foundation so you can sing with confidence.

  • Posture and Breath Support
  • Understanding Your Vocal Range
  • Basic Pitch Accuracy Exercises

Intermediate Harmonies: Singing Together

Ready to sing with others? This workshop dives into the art of harmony. You'll learn how to listen to other singers, find your part, and create beautiful blended chords as a group.

  • Introduction to Music Theory
  • Holding a Harmony Part
  • Blending and Vocal Dynamics

Advanced Performance: Own the Stage

This intensive workshop is for experienced singers looking to elevate their stage presence. We cover everything from microphone technique to storytelling through song, preparing you for compelling solo performances.

  • Advanced Microphone Control
  • Crafting a Performance
  • Connecting with an Audience
